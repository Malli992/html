Doraemon is a blue, robotic cat . He travels back in time to the present day to help a young boy named Nobita . Doraemon has a fourth-dimensional pocket from which he pulls out various gadgets to assist Nobita with his daily problems. Nobita's friends include Shizuka, Gian, and Suneo, who often join in Doraemon's adventures. The series explores themes of friendship, family,determination, and the impact of technology on society. Doraemon is a cultural icon in Japan and has gained international popularity, especially in Asia. The series inclues manga, anime series, films, video games, and merchandise. Doraemon's adventures with Nobita and friends are humorous, heartwarming, and sometimes touching.
